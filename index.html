About

I’m Lukas, a UX/UI designer with eight years spent between research, interface design, development and the messy bit in between.

I care about the details users never notice — the spacing that feels right, the flow that never makes you stop and think, the copy that says exactly enough. Good design, to me, is the absence of friction.

I work closely with engineers and founders, prototype early, and test often. Less polish for its own sake, more clarity that earns its place.
